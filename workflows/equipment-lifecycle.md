# Equipment Lifecycle Workflow

## Overview

Equipment assets move through several operational states during their lifecycle.

Tracking these states allows operations teams to maintain visibility into equipment availability and maintenance requirements.

## Lifecycle States

Active  
Maintenance  
Parked  
Assigned  
Unassigned  
Out of Service  
Retired  

## Operational Controls

Lifecycle states cannot be deleted to preserve historical data integrity.

State transitions are managed through controlled updates within the application interface.
