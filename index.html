Case Study

An asset lifecycle system, not just an equipment list.

EMP demonstrates centralized asset management through structured entities, lifecycle states, financial tracking concepts, governance controls, and operational views.

Problem

Equipment data can become fragmented across spreadsheets, invoices, assignments, and maintenance records.

Solution

A model-driven portal concept that centralizes equipment profiles, lifecycle status, invoices, and related operational details.

Technical Focus

Entity design, Dataverse-style relationships, lifecycle tracking, governance rules, and role-based system concepts.

Business Value

Improves visibility into asset status, ownership, cost context, and operational readiness.
